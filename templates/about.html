About Williamsburg Local News

Connecting our historic community through local journalism and civic engagement

Our Mission

Williamsburg Local News exists to strengthen our community by providing accurate, timely, and relevant local news coverage. We believe that informed citizens make stronger neighborhoods and that local journalism is essential to democratic participation.

We are committed to:

  • Covering city council meetings and local government decisions
  • Highlighting community events and local business news
  • Reporting on school board meetings and educational developments
  • Celebrating community achievements and local heroes
  • Providing a platform for civic discourse and engagement

Our Community Focus

Williamsburg is a unique community where history meets modern life. Our coverage reflects this diversity, from Historic District preservation efforts to new development projects, from Colonial Williamsburg events to local high school sports.

Historic Preservation

Covering efforts to preserve our colonial heritage while supporting modern community needs.

Education Coverage

Reporting on Williamsburg-James City County Schools and the College of William & Mary.

Coverage Areas

Local Government

City council meetings, county board sessions, and municipal decisions that affect daily life.

Business & Economy

Local business openings, economic development, and commercial district news.

Community Events

Festivals, concerts, fundraisers, and celebrations that bring our community together.

About Our Publisher

Williamsburg Local News is published by longtime community residents who believe in the power of local journalism to strengthen neighborhoods and inform civic participation. Our team combines professional journalism experience with deep local knowledge and community connections.

"A well-informed community is a strong community. We're here to make sure Williamsburg residents have the local news they need to participate fully in our democratic community."
